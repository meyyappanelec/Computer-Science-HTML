About Thanjavur Periya Kovil

Thanjai Periya Kovil also known as Brihaadeshwara Temple is a temple builded by Raja Raja Chozhan one of the most powerful king of the Chozha Dynasty. The Temple turned 1000 years old in 2010.The temple is part of the UNESCO World Heritage Site known as the “Great Living Chola Temples”.
